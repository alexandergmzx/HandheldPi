# TC-042 (power-loss durability) — PREP half, for the on-device dry run
# (docs/DEVICE_DRY_RUN.md §6). Completes a pick while offline and exits
# WITHOUT flushing: the ordered op chain (scan-location -> scan-article ->
# confirm) stays in the sqlite queue on purpose. Hard power-cut the unit
# right after this passes, boot, and verify the 3 ops survived.
# (Under pytest this just proves the chain queues without delivery.)

reset_queue
tick
scan OP:picker01
pin 1234
press a
expect_state IDLE

press a
expect_state GOTO_LOCATION

wms offline
scan LOC:A-01-03
expect_state SCAN_ARTICLE
scan ART:ART-SHIRT
expect_state SET_QUANTITY
press a
expect_state CONFIRMED
expect_queue 3
expect_dead 0
# no flush — power-cut here; the queue must survive the cut
